All about me

Who am I and how did I get here?

I am originally from a small Hungarian town, but I always felt I needed to see more of the world, so I moved around here and there. I always say “Home is where the heart is”. I have a seize-the-day attitude, and although have long-term plans in my mind, I feel it is super important to find happiness in everyday life. I graduated as a preschool teacher and worked in the field for almost 10 years, it gave me lots of love, but not the flexibility to live my dreams.
When I was considering a career change, the following things were in my mind:

  • Something that gives me a challenge!
  • Something I enjoy doing so much, time flies with it.
  • Something where I can use my creativity.
  • Something where I can constantly improve and keeps me motivated.
  • Something that gives me bigger flexibility in every sense.

I fell in love with coding, not only because it ticks all the boxes, but because I enjoy using my logic, and it gives me so much joy when I solve a problem. It is a whole side of me that as a teacher, I had less chance to explore, so I am very excited about the new challenges.
